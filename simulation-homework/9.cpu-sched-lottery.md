# 5: Can you make a version of the graph that is found in the chapter? What else would be worth exploring? How would the graph look with a stride scheduler?

## Graph for stride scheduler

- **Condition:**
  - the lengths of 2 jobs are equal.
  - tickets ratio is kept the same.
  - run order is kept the same.
- **Result:** the graph will be a horizontal line that is very close to 1 (but never equal).
- **Explain:**
  - stride scheduler is deterministic.
  - 2 jobs cannot complete at the same time.
